Fri, February 9, 2007 - 3:14 PM What a harrowing 20hrs it has been. We began prepping M for the EEG procedure yesterday by keeping him up until midnight. (easier said than done). Then, we had to wake up at 4 am this morning, no food, just water until his 10am appointment time. He was not thrilled by this at all. We spent the morning playing, coloring, and avoiding the subject of food as much as possible. Finally at 8:30, we went for a walk to kill time until we needed to leave at 9. It was cool; the tree trimmers were across the street in their big trucks and cherry pickers. They waved at M and he thought that was neat. We started up the street for our usual route and made it about 1 block before I spied a possum ahead on someone’s lawn. Not just a possum but a former possum missing a rather large chunk of midsection. Disemboweled as it were. I guess the coyotes are getting braver. ick. We turned right around and headed back home. Not only did I not have a way to keep track time, I wasn't quite feeling like walking anymore. Our instructions were to keep M awake from 4 am until his appointment. I knew the ride there would be difficult. Sure enough, he began to nod off almost immediately so I resorted to rolling the windows down and turning up the radio (through which I learned that the big rumor in gollywood is that anna nicole's son was the father of her baby). oy. reach back and tickle the leg. roll down the windows more. turn on the AC. none of it was working well. eyelids kept drooping. thankfully, children's isn't too far away from us but he was almost entirely asleep by the time I parked. open the door and he startles awake. wide awake. in fact, we seem to have hit that "second wind" I always wish I could find in myself. I spent the next hour in the waiting room with other parents staring incredulously at me. I'm guessing the looks were due to my boisterous (albeit cheerful) son charging around the waiting room laughing loudly (very loudly, try ear splitting) at the fish in the tank and the television playing the Disney channel. of course, when he wasn't busy doing those things, he was attempting to open the door and escape.
