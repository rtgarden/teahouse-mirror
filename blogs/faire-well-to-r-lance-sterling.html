3 Carol Williams May 18, 2010 at 06:34PM
It was back in 1969 or 1970, and believe it or not, Sterling and I got stopped by the cops in Venice, at 11:30 at night, illegally searched, and since they found a joint in my wallet, we were handcuffed, thrown in the squad car, and trucked off to our separate jails. Ironically, I was taken to the Van Nuys facility, and I had been an assistant to the architect who designed the bloody place while I worked for him. Sterling was pretty mad, you can imagine, but actually I'd brought the joint to share with him. As the cop lead me down the hallway, another passed us coming the other way. I was wearing a tie dye tanktop, without a bra. It Was the Sixties, after all.... "Awww.....how come You get all the luck," he commented with a grin. There's a lot more to the story, and I shall get to the rest of the details one of these days, but I'll never forget how Sterling described the mornings breakfast, and in particular the sausages. "Oh Carol," he said, shaking his head. "Breakfast was Terrible. Those sausages tasted like something the Warden scraped off his shoe."
