The light on my Lava lamp looks very interesting right now!
By [18d4mpa3n2i74] | December 25, 2007 | 0 comments
My pink oil with yellow wax lava lamp looks like some sort of insect. A giant grasshopper or something. Just thought all of you ex acid head would want to know, LOL. Okay, like wow man! I had so much fun on Sunday it's not even funny! I think I could get really used to working at Dicken's! And maybe by next year my feet will be ready for that sort of a beating again! God knows it was easier walking around all day for two days than it has been for years! Ten years ago there were times when it was too much to even walk more than an hour at a time! But I did most of my walking around on Saturday and went and watched all the shows on Sunday! I Saw naughty French Post Cards for the first time, ooh lala! And I saw J Paul's Magic show. I randomly went over to Mad Sals and listened to their versions of the 12 days of christmas, what a riot! I absolutely long for a male drag costume, I confess! But as usual, I can never decide if I want to go the artful dodger route, or the Top Hat and Tails route? I already have a Top Hat. I must confess that I have never bought a Pat's Hat in my life, only because I could neve make up my mind! he has so many cool one's that I want them all!!! However one of my good deeds for the weekend was to go around and give out some of the photos I have had just reprinted. I gave one to Casey and one to John Schulps for his brother. I got Davera's email and emailed her her's. I got to see my son and his adopted father for a few hours. And I got to hang out with one of my favorite Auntie mame characters, our Silas. *kiss*. Thanks girls coughing up tickets. I never would have been able to go without them this year. Usually I can afford to pay, this whole year I've been rather flush until these last two months! I've actually been supporting myself for the first time in my adult life!!! That is in a manner in which I have become accustomed at least, :) Yes, funds are low, but the light is returning and things will turn around. I went to a lovely Solstice Ritual on Friday night before going to Dicken's on Saturday, it was the first time I got to see Helen since we discovered she was Sally's sister. I've known her for a few years now through the Sonoma County pagan network's monthly gatherings. It was interesting to see how animated she got talking about Sally's disappearance and how her father has chosen to grieve and assume Sally is dead, but with the missing portfolio one wonders.....(dunt, dunt, dunt, dah!) Stay tuned for another chapter of the old renfaire mystery theater.....LOL. Well a shout out to Stacy, and Laura if she ever gets her butt on here! It was nice seeing you girls after all this time! And to my new (old?) buddies, Carrie, Kathi, Odie and Rory. and a special nod to J Paul, it was lovely to meet you in the street and then to have my friend take me to your show the next day. I didn't know it was YOUR show until you came out on stage! and Rydell it was awfully fun making eyes at you just after meeting your brother for the first time just minutes before! (maybe 20-40 or so!) Well off to beddy-by, tomorrow it's a trip to Sac'to for me and then time to start planning NYE!
