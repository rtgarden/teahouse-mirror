23 Greig Fors February 09, 2011 at 07:35PM
There are many people at faire that I wished I'd known better (some not so much).  Faire was always so hectic, and speaking for myself, my faire day was all week long and I would put in ten to twelve hour days during the week to catch up on inventory for my booth, then on the weekends, I would put in a twelve hour day, then I could cut loose in the evening.  Time was always short, but I crammed as much into the day as I possibly could.  Working Security (those years that I did), schmoozing was a wonderful part of the job.  Walking around faire with my partner Sarge (John Murphy), we knew so very many people, if not by name, then by sight.  Stefanie was one of those special people that when I saw her, I would smile.  When I worked the Tahoe Faire (Valhalla), it was so wonderful to know that she was there too.  She is missed.  Thank you Jessie for posting the link to those pics of her.  Even the few that show her as a patient and looking very sick.  In those few pics, her spirit shines through her illness.  She was one of the brighter stars that has sadly gone out.
