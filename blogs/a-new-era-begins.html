I've never quite gotten the whole 2-party system or the Electoral College, and think much of our governing system needs a total overhaul -- it is antiquated and doesn't even begin to address the concerns of the 21st Century. I agree with quite a bit of what Randy said, and yet, still feel surprisingly optimistic. Of course, my take on politics is that if we are patient, we tend to outlive any of the political regimes (a great lesson from working on the Deco Crew at the Renn Faires for 14 years -- the bosses came and went, and we just kept on throwing fru-fru up all over the place.) I see people being stirred up out of their apathy and working together, and even if it's just for the moment (and maybe only in the place I live!), it's like a breath of fresh air after living in a mouldering cellar for too many years.
