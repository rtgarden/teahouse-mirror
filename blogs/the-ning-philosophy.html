16 Eric Lethe November 27, 2007 at 06:35PM
More from research about Network Creators... Here's how to add administrators: "...Yep, you can add Administrators to help you manage your network. Just go to the Manage tab and click the "Members" link. Check the box next to the members you'd like to be admins and click the "Promote to Administrator" button..." Note this doesn't change the ownership of the Network, only adds people rights to people who can help with admin. BTW, unlike Tribe...you are not only Moderator, you are the "Network Creator" which means you own whatever copyright problems may arise...and also that if you ever wanted to transfer "Creator" status to another member of the collective (say if you weren't interested anymore or were unable to maintain the Network for health reasons) you would have to do so by surrendering your Ning ID to one of us because each Network can only be attached to one Ning ID. The Creators Forum explains that you can do this by deleting or changing all the personal info attached to your Ning ID and then disclosing your password and PIN to someone else. Unfortunately all the comments that ID has made will persist unless explicitly deleted. Deleting the ID destroys the Network.
