11 PatriciaBlanco November 24, 2007 at 06:47PM
Preston Ranch. Where to begin ? In the 1870s a Strong Woman Healer came to Cloverdale, CA to open a healing spa. I know no details of Madame Prestons' life before she came to California. She was a Faith Healer, and a believer in Nutrition, Pure Water and Exercise. At its' height, the Preston Coloony had around 500 people living there. Madam Preston built a beautiful house for herself, and many other structures. A lot of this is conjecture and hearsay on my part, I will admit. But I did live there briefly in the winter of 1978, and visited Edwin and Lisa for the next two decades or so, until they were forced to leave. After Madam Prestons' death, the property was uninhabited for a generation or so. Weeds covered the old graveyard on the hill where Madam was laid to rest, along with some of her followers. I believe it was in the late 60s that the heirs allowed some Artists to come in and try to do something with the genteel ruin, before it fell into worse repair. Some names are well known in this forun: Bob Thomas, Will Spires,Don Brown, Woodworkers, musicians, Flamenco dancers,and somehow it all came together for the Renaissance Faires. This Artists' colony succumbed to a fire in the 80s, and all of the beautiful Victorian buildings are now gone. But ... we could do it again elsewhere. Which is where MY version of "stability " would be. I can no longer physically tolerate a gypsy lifestyle, so my stability would be 10 acres with a farmhouse and workshop and "event" space. Gardens. Animals.Stages. Hootches.
