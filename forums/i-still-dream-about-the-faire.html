5 Murray then, now aka MAX March 12, 2009 at 03:17PM
i saw toby joe in my dream too... the night he passed. Laughing the good laugh. "fairmares" Adam Long and I used to call them. I used to have a lot of them, especially ones where I was arriving at a strange front gate, and trying to find a camping spot in strange camping areas, and always so many people that I dont know. Often these landscapes have been repeated later on at scenes like Oregon country faire and Earthdance and that such. Like walking along and suddenly its that same odd bend in the road with campsites everywhere, only this time I know exactly where I am. A strange dreamtime deja vu thing. I even wrote my dreams down for many years to confirm it to myself. I keep a dream book on the bed, and do the practice as much as I can. Appolonia does it also, and she reminds me that its good to even write down "i had a dream and i forgot it" as a way of keeping the practice going. Lucid dreaming is very cool, sometimes if you know you are dreaming you can jump up and fly around. I dont lucid dream as much as I used to, although its better than it was for awhile. I do however have superpowers in my dreams and I can do sorcerers tricks and make things appear and disappear and slip through the dream curtain from one place to another, and I have like alternate lives there and whole cities I live in with many of my pals. One of those places is very connected to our summer camping activities and many of you are there.
6 PatriciaBlanco March 12, 2009 at 06:58AM
I had a dream awhile back that I wanted to get hired again on the burlap crew, but hadn't been to a fair in years, so I wasn't sure if I would know anyone, that would put a good word in for me. Plus I wanted to get the perfect spot to set up my camp. I traveled many hours to get to the hiring day and was surprised to see many familiar faces, as I pulled onto the lot, which was a unfamiliar one I had never worked at, but I think it was southern Calif. I parked and got stopped by a couple young goons that told me I had to find another place to park, as I had parked in Howard Hanburgs spot. I was amazed to hear I at least knew one person. I moved my car and decided to find Howard for starters. As I walked through the lot I had many people say hi and welcomed me back, then I saw Debbie Dalton, she said she had a great camping site and if I hurried I could get the one next to her,that all our old friends were there, but it was several miles away, and I didn't want to take the chance on missing out on a job. I continued looking for Howard, then spotted some campers off to the side of offices, so I went and found a vacant spot, ran back got my car and took the spot. Venta was running burlap crew and she took one look at me and decided I was to old to be climbing around on the scaffolding, and said she wouldn't hire me, I was really bummed so decided I could work an ale stand gig, and Vetas was the man to talk to. I never found him, but I found Murray and he said I could help him at the gate taking tickets. So I got to be around the fair and camp and party like it was 1979. It was a very vivid dream and I woke up tired and happy. I would say I have at least one or two faire dreams a year, and some have been a bit x rated so I won’t share those, but will say in them I have got to be with several of the hotties, I was attracted to when I worked the faire.
