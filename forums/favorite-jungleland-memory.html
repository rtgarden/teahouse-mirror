21 Eric Lethe January 04, 2009 at 10:35PM
First time I set foot in Agoura Jungleland (remember, there was one up the canyon at Blackpoint, too)...I was chasing Roger Senn's daughter Jolene, who was about 10 years old and sassy as can be. I'd caught her one pre-Faire evening hanging upside down from the highest balcony of Mainstage, which was still getting fixed up and deco'd for that year. I was concerned she was going to fall and kill herself, so yelled at her to get down from there. She told me in her cute little girl's voice to go fuck myself, and then took off running for the protection of her Dad. Impressed with her general vocabulary (and at that point mostly unaware of Roger except as the guy who drove Orange Crush)...I thought it would be worthwhile to have a word with him. So I followed her. It was after working hours, and there was a party happening. Nitrous canister with somebody sucking the tube and turning blue. Loud music. Dogs off their leashes. I found Roger (at least I assumed it was him, since she was whispering in his ear) and said my piece about the stage being unsafe for kids to hang off of, etc. I didn't ask how his sweet little daughter had learned to curse like a sailor...one good look at him pretty much told me everything I needed to know. What surprised me was that he was very respectful and apologized on her behalf. He called me Ma'am (I was 23 years old). He made Jolene apologize. He offered me a beer or some other refreshment, but I was in a big hurry to get the heck off that hill, since most of the other half-naked sweaty crew guys were by this time hooting cat calls in our direction. He first asked me out about a week later...
