11 [2fmhvf3f5ehyz] September 08, 2008 at 09:34AM
Thanks for posting, Rory. : ( Just got home from opening weekend, where I heard the news this morning. Hit me pretty hard. I started my faire *career* at The Rose and Stag - spent 6 seasons there with Ruth and Steve from ' 87 until ' 92, their final year at Black Point. She was a magnificent lady, teacher, boss and friend. I recall the zombie-like stares of all of us Rose and Staggers during the ' 93 run - walking around, not knowing quite where *home* was anymore. We were a lost and sad lot without Ruth and Steve. That feeling came crashing back on me today at Casa. There are very few of us that still do faire. I made my way to Glynnis (now Papgeorgacopolis) and we clung to one another for a wee bit. She was working and couldn't stop. I, of course, made my way to an ale stand and filled my tankard with beer. That is truly what Ruth would have wanted. I plan to do that over and over and over again, you know, for Ruth ; ) Thanks again, Rory, for spreading the news by starting this discussion - I may never shut up on this topic ; )
