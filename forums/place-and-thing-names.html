22 Greg "Grego" Dana February 09, 2009 at 05:41AM
Swomper is actually spelled Swamper... A swamper is basically an assistant. It is an old trucking and logging term but it also refers to the ground worker for dozers doing land clearing. When I was in the army, I operated a dozer and had a swamper that watched for hazards and helped maintain it. Our inmate hand crews have an inmate that is assigned the swamper position and acts as the Fire Captains crew assistant and is responsible for transport and equipment maintenance.
