For me, my initiation was all due to Elinore Harold (now Peterson), who never let on what we were doing or why we were sneaking off (which was her m.o., she did this with every activity my first year). My first year was '84, that year dress up for the coctail walk was in the Prop Shed Loft, she never said a word to me, but had a ready made a costume for me. She snuck me back to the Prop Shed via the highway, and made sure no one dressing in the loft let on to what was coming. It was the same thing for the Giggle Fest that year. She made sure that neither Jessie Horack or Don Mills (or anyone else for that matter) spilled the beans about that weekend's activities. I can't tell you how greatfull I am to this day for that. It was all that much more exciting.
